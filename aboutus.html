Who We Are

At CodeSpark KE, we are committed to providing innovative and accessible technology education to children across Kenya. Our mission is to empower students with the coding, robotics, and IT skills needed to thrive in a digital world. With a strong focus on hands-on, practical learning, we aim to make tech education fun, engaging, and impactful. We are a passionate team of tech enthusiasts and educators who believe that the future of Kenya lies in the hands of its youth. Through our interactive workshops, after-school programs, holiday camps, and custom courses, we offer a wide range of services designed to nurture creativity, problem-solving, and critical thinking in young minds.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Our mission is to deliver high-quality tech education to children, equipping them with the necessary skills to succeed in the digital world. Through interactive, hands-on experiences, we strive to inspire creativity, innovation, and critical thinking.

Our Mission

Our vision is to become a leading provider of coding, robotics, and IT education in Kenya, empowering students across the country to lead the way in technology and innovation.
