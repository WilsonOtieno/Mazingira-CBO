Who We Are

Mazingira Urban Networking is a grassroots environmental and climate change organization based in Roysambu Sub-County, Nairobi, Kenya. Established in February 2024, we are dedicated to creating sustainable urban communities through initiatives in environmental cleanup, tree planting, and waste management. Our organization was founded out of the urgent need to address urban environmental degradation and to foster collective action toward greener, cleaner, and more resilient cities.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Envisions building a community where all people have the opportunity and capacity to direct, economic, social goals and wellbeing of an environmental resources towards sustainability outcomes that improve the lives of the community members.

Our Mission

To Networking with other organizations in order to improve the polluted environment in the new order to improve the welfare of marginalized children, youth women and elderly persons in the community.
