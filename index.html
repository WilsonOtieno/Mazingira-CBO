Our Story

Since its founding in 2024, Mazingira Urban Networking has been steadily growing as a grassroots force for environmental action and sustainable urban development. What began as a small community initiative in Roysambu, Nairobi, has evolved into an active organization championing environmental cleanup, tree planting, and waste management programs. Through collaboration with local communities, schools, and partners, we continue to raise awareness and inspire collective responsibility toward protecting our environment. Our journey is driven by a shared vision—to create cleaner, greener, and more livable urban spaces for generations to come.
