Our Story

Since its founding in 2024, CodeSpark KE has been gradually emerging as a leading force in empowering learners through coding, robotics, and IT programs. We provide innovative education and training, partnering with schools, communities, and organizations across Kenya. Headquartered in Nairobi, our reach extends through collaborations with schools, boot camps, and hackathons, inspiring learners to embrace technology and innovation.
