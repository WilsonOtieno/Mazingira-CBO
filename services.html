Our Services

Environmental Cleanup Campaigns

We organize regular community cleanup drives in collaboration with local residents, schools, and organizations. These campaigns aim to improve hygiene, enhance aesthetics, and promote community pride in maintaining clean urban spaces.

Tree Planting and Urban Greening

Mazingira Urban Networking leads tree planting initiatives focused on restoring green cover within urban environments. We identify planting sites, source indigenous tree species, and ensure long-term maintenance to combat air pollution and urban heat while supporting biodiversity.

Waste Management and Recycling Programs

We offer training and awareness programs on proper waste segregation, recycling, and disposal. Through workshops, educational materials, and partnerships with local leaders, we empower communities to reduce waste and generate income through recycling and composting.

Wet Waste and Composting Solutions

Our team promotes sustainable wet waste management by converting organic waste into compost or using vermiculture (red worms) to produce nutrient-rich material for farming and poultry feed. This reduces landfill waste while supporting small-scale agricultural ventures.

Environmental Education and Capacity Building

We provide educational programs, workshops, and training sessions to equip members and community groups with skills in environmental conservation, waste management, and climate resilience. We also collaborate with government institutions and partners to enhance local knowledge and innovation.

Community Empowerment and Support Initiatives

Beyond environmental work, Mazingira Urban Networking serves as a support forum for members, promoting financial, social, emotional, and mental well-being. We encourage self-reliance and the sharing of resources to uplift the economic and social status of our members and communities.
